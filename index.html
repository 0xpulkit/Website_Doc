Our Story

Dr. V Singh Memorial Centre is conceptualized by Dr. Kshitij Aditya & Dr. Pankhuri Mittal in the memory of their father Late Dr. V. Singh, who was the first specialist doctor in the region serving from 1973-2006. He was an excellent ophthalmologist and surgeon and an even better human being. His dedication towards his work and compassion towards the community is still unmatched. This Institute has been set up following his ideals, to provide world class quality and affordable care to all.
We provide comprehensive Eye & ENT Services with best of super specialist doctors in our panel, with cutting edge surgical & diagnostic facilities. Thus, ensuring people of this region do not have to travel far for the treatment.

Learn About Me

Dr. Kshitij Aditya (Director) He completed his MBBS from prestigious T.N. Medical College & Nair Hospital, Mumbai (2005-2010). He then did D.O. from Guru Nanak Eye Centre (Maulana Azad Medical College, New Delhi) and D.N.B from BSAH Medical College, New Delhi (2011-2015). He also obtained prestigious fellowships from U.K. [F.I.C.O London], [FRCS Glasgow] as well as Phaco fellowship from MM JEI. He did his ling term Surgical Retina Fellowship from Biratnagar Eye Hospital, Nepal (2017-2019). He worked as Retina Consultant In charge at Lahan, Nepal and Varanasi. He has more than 5000 Retina procedures to his credit with a keen interest in ROP management services. He has many publications & national as well as international presentations in his name.
